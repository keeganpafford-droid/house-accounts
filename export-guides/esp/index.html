ESP / ASI Guide

Export Customer History from ESP / ASI

Export customer order history directly from the Orders section inside ESP.

Estimated time: 2–3 minutes
House Accounts Recommendation

Export approximately 3 years of customer order history whenever possible. More historical data allows House Accounts to identify stronger buying patterns, repeat opportunities, and seasonal trends.

Export steps

Step 1

Open Orders.

Step 2

Filter the report for approximately the last 3 years.

Step 3

Select the orders you want included.

Step 4

Choose Export to Excel.

Step 5

Upload the exported spreadsheet into House Accounts.

Recommended fields

✓ Customer / Company Name✓ Project or Order Name✓ Order Date✓ Revenue

Pipedrive Prospect Guide

Export a Prospect List from Pipedrive

Export Organizations or People from a filtered list view or the Export data area.

Estimated time: 2–3 minutes

Only users with the required export permissions can export records, and visibility rules still apply.

House Accounts Recommendation

Export the companies you actively want to pursue. Company name or website is usually enough for House Accounts to begin researching relevant business signals.

Contact names, titles, and public profile links improve the quality of person-level research when available.

Export Steps

Step 1

Open Organizations or People in Pipedrive.

Step 2

Apply a filter so the list contains only the records you want House Accounts to research.

Step 3

Use the gear icon to choose the useful columns displayed in the list.

Step 4

Open the list actions menu and choose Export filter results.

Step 5

Choose CSV when prompted.

Step 6

Download the generated file.

Step 7

Upload the CSV into Prospect Intelligence.

Recommended Fields

✓ Company Name✓ Website / Domain

Helpful if available

A perfect dataset is not required. House Accounts can begin with a company name alone.

House Accounts Tip

Do not worry if your prospect list is incomplete. Company name or website is usually enough to begin researching business signals. Contact names, titles, and public profile links simply improve research quality.

Pipedrive Pro Tip

Export Organizations when company-level research is the priority. Export access depends on your Pipedrive permissions and record visibility.

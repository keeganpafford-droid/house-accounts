Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

House Accounts helps promotional products distributors analyze account lists and identify reasons to reach out to customers. This policy explains the types of information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

We may collect contact information such as name, work email, company, role, CRM/ERP system, and account list details uploaded by users.

How we use information

We use information to provide account analysis, save dashboards, monitor accounts for new signals, send weekly opportunity alerts, improve the product, and respond to support requests.

Uploaded account data

Uploaded account data may include company names, contacts, order history, revenue, dates, and other columns included by the user. This data is used to generate House Accounts insights and ongoing monitoring.

Third-party services

House Accounts may use service providers for hosting, database storage, email delivery, AI analysis, web search, webpage extraction, analytics, and payments.

Sharing

We do not sell customer account data. We may share information with service providers as needed to operate the product, comply with law, or protect the service.

Data requests

To request deletion or correction of account information, contact hello@houseaccounts.ai.

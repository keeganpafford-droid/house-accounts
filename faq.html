Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about House Accounts before uploading your account list.

How does House Accounts work?

Upload your customer or house-account list. House Accounts reads the historical order data, looks for follow-up and repeat buying patterns, scans public business activity, and turns those signals into prioritized reasons to reach out.

Does House Accounts replace my CRM?

No. House Accounts is not trying to replace Commonsku, Facilis, Antera, HubSpot, Salesforce, or your ERP. It sits above your data and helps answer one question: who should I contact this week, and why?

Where do business signals come from?

Business signals come from public sources such as company websites, careers pages, press releases, event pages, news articles, and other publicly available web results. We show source context whenever possible so reps can verify the opportunity.

How often do you scan accounts?

The product is built around weekly monitoring. Once an account list is uploaded, House Accounts can continue watching for new reasons to reach out and send a weekly opportunity email.

Can I upload multiple account lists?

Yes. New uploads can be saved as new account lists so teams can update their data over time without losing prior signal history.

Is my customer data secure?

Your data is used only to generate insights for your organization. It is not sold, shared, or mixed with other distributors. See the Data Security page for the full plain-English promise.

Do you support Commonsku, Facilis, Antera, Essent, and other systems?

Today, House Accounts works from CSV exports. Native CRM and ERP integrations are on the roadmap, but the current goal is to make upload-based monitoring valuable before adding heavier integrations.

What happens after I upload my CSV?

Your account list is analyzed, saved, and monitored. You get a dashboard showing today’s best reasons to reach out, account intelligence, and new business activity discovered since the last scan.

Can my team share one account?

For the beta, yes. Team management and seat-level permissions will come later as usage grows.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Once paid plans are active, subscriptions will be managed through Stripe and can be cancelled without a long-term contract.

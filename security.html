Data Security

Built to handle customer account data responsibly.

House Accounts analyzes account lists, order history, and public business signals to help sales teams decide who to contact. We keep the product lean, but we take customer data seriously.

Account data

Uploaded account data is used to generate House Accounts insights, monitor future business activity, and power weekly alerts.

Public signals

Business activity signals come from public web sources and are displayed with source context whenever available.

Access

Production secrets and third-party API keys are stored as server-side environment variables. Service-role credentials are not exposed in browser code.

AI usage

AI is used to classify and summarize potential reasons to reach out. The product is designed to support sales judgment, not replace it.

Current beta posture

House Accounts is an early-stage product. We are not currently claiming SOC 2, HIPAA, or enterprise compliance certifications. Larger customers should contact us for security review before uploading sensitive or regulated data.

Contact

Security questions can be sent to security@houseaccounts.ai.
